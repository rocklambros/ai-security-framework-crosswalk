## Structural invariance

The discriminative metric (MRR / Recall@5 / ROC-AUC) is computed on the **composite score**, which is a weighted blend of bridge / semantic / keyword / function-match signals. The (direct, related_primary) thresholds are consumed only by the downstream classification step that buckets edges into direct/related/none. Therefore swapping thresholds cannot move the masked composite or the distractor cells, and per-pair MRR delta is structurally zero (observable below as `delta ≈ 0` for every pair).
